\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
An \mbox{\hyperlink{classcom_1_1thealgorithms_1_1maths_1_1_armstrong}{Armstrong}} number is equal to the sum of the cubes of its digits. For example, 370 is an \mbox{\hyperlink{classcom_1_1thealgorithms_1_1maths_1_1_armstrong}{Armstrong}} number because 3$\ast$3$\ast$3 + 7$\ast$7$\ast$7 + 0$\ast$0$\ast$0 = 370. An \mbox{\hyperlink{classcom_1_1thealgorithms_1_1maths_1_1_armstrong}{Armstrong}} number is often called Narcissistic number.
