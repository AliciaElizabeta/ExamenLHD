\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A \href{https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Double-ended_queue}{\texttt{ deque}} is short for a double ended queue pronounced \char`\"{}deck\char`\"{} and sometimes referred to as a head-\/tail linked list. A deque is a data structure based on a doubly linked list, but only supports adding and removal of nodes from the beginning and the end of the list.
