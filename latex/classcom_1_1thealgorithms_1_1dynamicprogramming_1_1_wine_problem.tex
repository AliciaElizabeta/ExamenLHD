Imagine you have a collection of N wines placed next to each other on the shelf. The price of ith wine is pi(\+Prices of different wines are different). Because wine gets better every year supposing today is year 1, on year y the price would be y$\ast$pi i.\+e y times the value of the initial year. You want to sell all wines but you have to sell one wine per year. One more constraint on each year you are allowed to sell either leftmost or rightmost wine on the shelf.
