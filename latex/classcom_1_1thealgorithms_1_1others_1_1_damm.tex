\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{classcom_1_1thealgorithms_1_1others_1_1_damm}{Damm}} algorithm is a check digit algorithm that detects all single-\/digit errors and all adjacent transposition errors. It was presented by H. Michael \mbox{\hyperlink{classcom_1_1thealgorithms_1_1others_1_1_damm}{Damm}} in 2004. Essential part of the algorithm is a quasigroup of order 10 (i.\+e. having a 10 Ã— 10 Latin square as the body of its operation table) with the special feature of being weakly totally anti-\/symmetric. \mbox{\hyperlink{classcom_1_1thealgorithms_1_1others_1_1_damm}{Damm}} revealed several methods to create totally anti-\/symmetric quasigroups of order 10 and gave some examples in his doctoral dissertation.
